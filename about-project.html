About Our Project

Our goal is to experience the old nostalgic days of Minecraft console, we want to bring it back, we are going to be playing all the updates and you can join us too, in order for it to feel like the old days we need a community, we are inviting you to either just tag along and play, become a creator and make YouTube videos or stream your content, you can find a list of official creators of the event (if you are a creator you can apply to be an official creator).

To find out how to experience the old Minecraft, be sure to check out our tutorials.
